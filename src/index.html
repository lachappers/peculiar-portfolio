Who I am

I am a First-Generation, Low-Income (FGLI), Neurodivergent, Female Leader in Technology and Business.

My unconventional background, breadth of experience and eternally curious brain makes me great at solving puzzles and finding overlooked connections.

I care deeply about making the world safer, fairer and more compassionate. For this to happen, I believe that access to, and an understanding of, data and technology must become universal.
