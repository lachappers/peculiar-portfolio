My Experience

I believe that everyone should have the technology, information and support they need to make a difference in the world.

Understanding technology and data is essential for our future, and requires a whole bunch of skills that many of us never learnt at school.

I began my career in consulting, working in cybersecurity, data analysis and technology implementation with global banking clients.

Curious about the gaps between technology and business, I diversified my work into technical business strategy and operations, then attended a top MBA program in 2018.

Since then, I've been working in big tech and senior leadership, building businesses, products, and teams during a time of unprecedented change.
