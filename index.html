My Experience

12+ years

helping businesses to use technology and data to solve problems, explore opportunities and make teams happier and more effective.

I've spent the last decade interpreting the peculiar dynamics between business needs, technical capability and human-to-human communication; helping businesses define and build the right strategies, with the right tech.

I believe that an understanding of technology and data is essential for any business to survive into our future. That's a whole bunch of skills, and a whole way of thinking that many of us weren't lucky enough to pick up in our school days!

I started my career in consulting with KPMG in 2012, working with global banks on Cyber Security and Data Analysis projects, branching out into technical business strategy and operations.

I saw how often technology is overlooked or misunderstood by "business people", and how often the needs of business are overlooked by or inaccessible to "techies".

Curious about how to bridge this gap, in 2018 I attended one of the best MBA programmes in the world. This launched me into the world of big tech and senior leadership, where I've been building businesses, products and teams over a time of unprecidented change.
